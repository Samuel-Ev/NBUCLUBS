What We Do

The Entrepreneurship Club at NBU inspires and equips students to become visionary leaders and changemakers. Through mentorship, innovation programs, and real-world challenges, we nurture the next generation of entrepreneurs.

Business Idea Incubation & Mentorship

We guide students from concept to startup launch through mentorship and innovation labs.

Financial Literacy & Startup Training

Learn about budgeting, investment, and financial management for sustainable entrepreneurship.

Innovation & Pitch Challenges

Participate in campus pitch contests where creativity meets business strategy.

Networking Events & Entrepreneur Meetups

Connect with business leaders, startup founders, and fellow innovators to share insights.
